## 🎯 Challenges, quizzes & labs

Live at `/community/challenges`. Three flavours:

- **🎯 Challenge** — open-ended brief, you ship a writeup + (optional) proof URL
- **🧩 Quiz** — quick-fire questions on a topic
- **🧪 Lab** — guided hands-on with a real artefact to produce

### How they work
- Every challenge has a **timer** (24h, 48h, 7d are common). The countdown is live on the card and the detail page.
- Each challenge has a **point value** (typically 50 pts) and an **optional badge** awarded on win.
- One **submission** per challenge per member. Markdown writeup + optional proof link (repo, demo, screenshot URL).
- Admin grades within ~48h. You'll see status update on the challenge page.
- **AI tools welcome.** Original thinking is what wins — don't paste raw model output.

### What you earn
- ✅ **Approved**: full points credited to your **all-time** + **monthly** totals, plus the badge if attached
- ❌ **Rejected**: zero points, but you get a note explaining what was missing
- ⏸ **Pending**: waiting on grading

### Spotlight
Top submissions appear on the challenge detail page (top 5 by score). Want to be there? Show your reasoning, your trade-offs, and one thing you'd do differently next time.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I un-member?**
Yes — reply to any email and ask. Your profile gets removed; subscriber email kept on the newsletter list (you can unsubscribe separately).

**Q: Can I change my handle?**
Not in v1. Handles are auto-generated from your name with a 4-char suffix. If yours is awkward, email and we'll fix it manually.

**Q: I missed a challenge — can I submit late?**
No. Timers are real. New challenges drop weekly.

**Q: Can I use AI to do challenges?**
Yes, freely. But submit *your* thinking, not the model's output. Graders can usually tell.

**Q: Where's the leaderboard?**
Members-only, coming in Phase 3 (Builder Points are already accumulating). Top 3 each month will spotlight publicly on the community page.

**Q: How do I get a skill badge?**
Win a challenge tagged with that track. Badge slug must match (e.g. `cyber-apprentice` for cyber-track challenges).

**Q: Can I run a workshop for members?**
Yes — DM Devashish on Discord with a 3-bullet pitch. If approved, you get a feature post + your own paid cohort with 100% revenue share.
